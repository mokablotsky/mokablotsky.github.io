mo kablotsky is a wishful thinker

hi! my name is mo kablotsky, and i've decided that art is a large part of my life - so large, i decided to attend art school and develop a formal portfolio on this website you're on. before you ever mention me: i'm non-binary, and i use they/them pronouns.

i'm from a tiny farm town in massachusetts, so small that everyone knows everyone's name, birthday, and entire family lineage. that sense of community and support raised me to become the artist i am today!

i work primarily on adobe photoshop, but i dabble in charcoals, pencils of all sorts, and acrylic paint on canvas. i enjoy dreaming up the most mythical of beasts - but you'll also catch me working on figure studies and observational drawing.

send me a kindly worded message mo.kablotsky@gmail.com
